This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on the website www.andreapiani.com, which types of cookies are present, and how you can manage or disable them.

In short: www.andreapiani.com is a purely static website hosted on a content delivery network. It has no backend application, no database and no server-side processing. The Site sets a single technical cookie (cookie_consent) to remember your banner choice, performs no analytics and no tracking, and uses no profiling cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that visited websites send to the user's device (computer, smartphone, tablet), where they are stored to be retransmitted to the same websites on the next visit.

In general, cookies can be used to:

  • ✓ Remember your preferences and settings
  • ✓ Improve the browsing experience
  • ✓ Make the website work properly

On this Site, cookies are used only to remember your cookie banner choice (see §2). They are not used to collect statistics or to track you.

2. Types of Cookies Used

🔧 Necessary Technical Cookies (DO NOT require consent)

These are essential cookies for the website to function. Without these cookies, some functionalities cannot be provided. According to the Italian Data Protection Authority Provision no. 229/2014, they do not require user consent.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Stores your cookie preferences (consent/refusal) 12 months

Note: the Site is served as static files and does not use any server-side session cookies. cookie_consent is the only cookie set by the Site.

💾 Local storage — technical UI preferences (no consent required)

The Site stores a small number of technical user-interface preferences in your browser's localStorage. These are not cookies, are never transmitted to any server, and contain no tracking identifiers. They exist solely to remember your display choices between visits.

Key Purpose Scope
darkMode Remembers your light/dark theme preference Local only, never transmitted

📊 Analytics and profiling cookies — NONE

The Site collects no statistics at all. There is no analytics service — neither first-party nor third-party (e.g. Google Analytics) — no tracking, no event logging and no collection or logging of visitor IP addresses by the Site. Accordingly, no analytical or profiling cookies are set. The cookie banner is retained purely for transparency and to record your preference. Should any analytics or profiling tool ever be introduced, this Cookie Policy will be updated and explicit consent under Art. 6.1.a GDPR will be requested beforehand.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Technical Cookie (cookie_consent): Controller's legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f GDPR) — does not require consent (Italian DPA Provision 229/2014).
  • Analytical / Profiling Cookies: none are used. Were any to be introduced in the future, they would require the User's explicit consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR) via the cookie banner.

5. How We Manage Your Consent

When accessing the website, you see a cookie banner that lets you record your choice. Since the Site uses only one technical cookie and no tracking, accepting or rejecting does not change the way your browsing data is processed; the banner is provided for full transparency:

  • ✅ Accept (records your preference)
  • ❌ Reject non-essential cookies (no tracking is performed in any case)

Your consent is stored for 12 months via the technical cookie cookie_consent. You can modify your preferences at any time using the "Manage Cookies" link at the bottom of the page.

6. How to Disable Cookies

6.1 Via the Website Banner

Use our cookie banner (on first access) or click on "Manage Cookies" in the footer to modify your preferences.

6.2 Via Browser Settings

You can disable cookies directly from your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Block all cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

⚠️ Warning: Completely disabling cookies may cause the cookie banner to reappear on every visit, as the Site can no longer remember your stored preference.

6.3 Analytics opt-out

No analytics tools (first-party or third-party) are active on the Site (see §2), so there is nothing to opt out of. Should any analytics service be introduced in the future, this Cookie Policy will be updated and an appropriate opt-out mechanism will be provided.

7. Cookies and Mobile Devices

If you browse from smartphone/tablet, you can manage cookies via:

  • iOS (Safari): Settings → Safari → Privacy & Security → Block cookies
  • Android (Chrome): Settings → Site settings → Cookies

8. Cookie Duration

The Site stores only the following items:

  • Persistent cookie: cookie_consent — kept for 12 months, then renewed on your next choice.
  • Local storage (technical UI preferences): e.g. darkMode — kept on the device until you clear your browser storage. Never transmitted.
