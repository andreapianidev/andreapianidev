2. Our Guiding Principle: No Personal Data Is Collected

The "Walkie Talkie" application operates on a strict "zero data collection" principle. We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) or sensitive data from our users.

This means we have no access to:

  • Personal Identifiers: Such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number.
  • Account Information: The App does not require user registration or the creation of an account. Your use of the App is entirely anonymous.
  • Location Data: The App does not track or store your geographical location (e.g., via GPS).
  • Device or User Identifiers: We do not collect unique identifiers like the UDID, IMEI, or advertising identifiers (IDFA).
  • Content of Communications: The content of your voice messages is never accessible to us.

3. How The App Functions: Offline Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Technology

The App's functionality is powered by Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework. This technology facilitates direct, serverless communication between nearby devices using a combination of Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Direct.

Key characteristics of this privacy-centric architecture include:

  • No Central Servers: Voice messages are transmitted directly from one user's device to another. They are never routed through or stored on any external servers managed by Immaginet Srl or any third-party entity.
  • Proximity-Based Discovery: The App discovers and connects only to other devices in your immediate physical vicinity, creating a temporary, local, and private network for your conversation.
  • Ephemeral Communication: Voice data exists only for the brief moment of transmission between devices. It is not recorded or stored permanently on the device by the App after the communication ends.

4. Automatically Processed Technical Data (Local Processing Only)

To ensure the App operates correctly and provides a stable user experience, it may need to access certain anonymous, non-personal technical data. This data processing occurs exclusively on your device and is never transmitted, logged, or shared with us or any third party.

This local data includes:

  • Device Model & OS Version: To ensure compatibility and adapt the user interface appropriately.
  • Connectivity Status: To manage the state of the Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connections for the P2P link.

This information is essential for the App's internal operations but is not used for analytics, tracking, or any other purpose.

5. Application Permissions and Justification

The App requests a minimal set of permissions that are strictly necessary to enable its core features. We only request access when a feature is used, and the associated data is handled as described below.

  • Microphone Access:
    Purpose: To capture your voice when you choose to send a walkie-talkie message.
    Data Handling: The audio stream is captured in real-time, encrypted, and transmitted directly to the connected peer device. No audio is ever recorded, saved, or stored on your device's filesystem or in the cloud.
  • Bluetooth & Local Network (Wi-Fi) Access:
    Purpose: This is essential for the Multipeer Connectivity framework to discover nearby devices running the App and to establish a secure, direct data link for communication.
    Data Handling: These permissions are used solely to manage the local P2P network connection. No data about your network configuration, your IP address, or other connected devices is logged or transmitted externally.

6. Data Security

We rely on the robust, industry-leading security protocols built into Apple's iOS and its development frameworks to protect your communications.

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data transmitted between devices via the Multipeer Connectivity framework is automatically protected with strong, native encryption (such as WPA2 for Wi-Fi and standard Bluetooth encryption protocols). This ensures your voice communications are secure and shielded from eavesdropping.
  • No Tracking or Analytics: The App is completely free of any third-party tracking scripts, advertising SDKs, or analytics tools. We do not monitor your behavior, usage patterns, or session data.

However, because the "Walkie Talkie" app does not collect, store, or process any of your personal data, these rights are not directly applicable. There is no personal data held by us that you would need to access, correct, or delete. Your privacy is protected by design through data minimization.
